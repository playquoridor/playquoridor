Joining the community

PlayQuoridor is a free/libre, no-ads, open source Quoridor server powered by volunteers and donations.

We're building a vibrant space for Quoridor enthusiasts to connect, compete, and enjoy the game in real time. Keeping PlayQuoridor running smoothly requires maintaining servers, improving features, and ensuring a great experience for everyone. We rely on support from players like you to make this possible. If you enjoy playing on PlayQuoridor, please consider supporting us with a donation—every contribution helps us keep the platform online and growing!

Contributing as a developer

Quoridor is more than just a game—it's a growing community, and you can be a part of shaping its future! While we may not have the expertise of Lichess, our goal is to become its equivalent for Quoridor, offering a free and open platform for everyone to play.

We'd love your help! The platform welcomes contributions from developers, designers, and testers. Whether you want to improve features, fix bugs, or enhance the user experience, your skills can make a real impact. Check out our GitHub, and let’s build an amazing online Quoridor community together!
